Recipes

Below you can browse recipes that use wild ingredients found in Denmark. You can sort recipes by season, type of plant, or alphabetically. Click on a recipe to see how you can cook with wild ingredients. Every recipe offers suggestions for at least one alternative wild ingredient, in case you don’t come across the ingredients you hope to while foraging. Read about the chef who wrote the recipe and print it if you don't feel like bringing your computer into the kitchen.
